Elevator Pitch

For years, drivers have been dissatisfied of finding an open parking spot only to find out that they have wasted 5 minutes driving in circles finding no open parking spot. And according to telegraph.co.uk/news it takes "the average motorist 5.9 mins to spend looking for a space, adding up to a total of 90.5 hours - or four days - spent searching for a parking spot over the year." What if there was a way to tell whether a spot is open. With our app we will be able to show a grid view of the parking lot for what spots are open or filled.
